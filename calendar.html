At my home, a diverse collection of Tamil language calendars can always be found. These calendars are acquired from various sources like Tamil grocery stores, bakeries, temples, and saree stores. This specific one is from a saree store that my family frequents. I have always been drawn to the aesthetic appeal of the calendar pages, and whenever a month ends, I would ask my appamma, who usually keeps the calendar in her room, if I could have the pages. I enjoyed drawing on the back and pretending to circle dates. These calendars have always been an irresistible collectible to me.
